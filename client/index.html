The GPA app is designed to help student calculate their semester with ease. Students can now calculate their Grade Point Average (GPA) in few seconds by just inputting the required data.

HOW TO USE

  1. Choose a grade scale which can be either 4 or 5, depending on your school.
  2. Enter the Course title (Optional) but encouraged for ease of usage.
  3. Enter the Unit of the courses inputted.
  4. Enter the grade.
  5. If you need more rows, please click the “add more courses” icon.
  6. Click Calculate result.
  7. Your result is shown below.
